Official 2022 Gubernatorial General Election Results for Prince George's County

By Jess Daninhirsch

The last election to select a governor and lieutenant governor in the state of Maryland occurred in November of 2022. Prince George's County's votes were overwhelmingly Democratic, with over 80% of the vote going to candidates Wes Moore and Aruna Miller. A majority of votes were cast in person on election day, followed by mail-in ballots. Although, a lot of people also participated in early voting, and fewer people participated in provisional voting. The state as a whole swung Democratic as well, withover 60% of the vote going to Moore and Miller across all counties.
