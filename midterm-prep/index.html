Jess Daninhirsch

Visual Journalist

Hailing from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Jess Daninhirsch is a sophomore journalism major and history minor at the University of Maryland. She is a visual journalist who loves photography, videography, writing, and broadcast. Outside of Merrill, she is in the Jewish a cappella group Kol Sasson and Avirah, the Israeli dance company. She is a photography intern with the UMD Office of Marketing & Communications.
